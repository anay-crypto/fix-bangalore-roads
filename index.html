Fix Bangalore Roads

Awareness. Action. Change.

The Problem

Bangalore is facing serious road problems due to potholes, poor drainage, heavy traffic, and repeated construction work. These issues affect safety, transportation, and daily life.

Main Causes

  • Heavy monsoon rains
  • Poor road construction quality
  • Repeated digging by utility departments
  • Lack of proper maintenance

Affected Areas (Examples)

Area Problem
Outer Ring Road Large potholes and waterlogging
Silk Board Junction Traffic congestion and road damage
Hebbal Uneven roads and safety risks

What Can Be Done?

  • Use better construction materials
  • Improve drainage systems
  • Coordinate between departments
  • Fix roads properly instead of temporary repairs
